About us

A small studio with a focus on craft

Bykdg was founded by Ali Büyükdağ as a platform to ship independently-developed mobile applications. We ship under a single developer identity on Google Play and bring each product to life with the same attention to detail — from user interface down to backend reliability.

Our apps share a common philosophy: solve one real problem well, protect user privacy, and keep the experience simple.
